Why this matters in Florida

Travertine around Jacksonville pools is exposed to rain, UV, moisture, sunscreen, organic debris, and frequent barefoot traffic. That combination can affect appearance, staining, and maintenance demands much faster than many homeowners expect.

FAQs

Common questions

Short answers for homeowners comparing options, timing, and next steps.

Should travertine around pools usually be sealed?

Many travertine pool decks benefit from sealing, especially when homeowners want help with stain resistance, easier maintenance, and a more finished look.

Will sealing make travertine slippery?

Product selection and finish choice matter. A professional should match the sealer to the surface use and slip-awareness needs.

Can travertine be pressure washed aggressively?

That is usually not the right approach. Overly aggressive cleaning can affect appearance and can be harder on the stone and joints.

What stains travertine most often?

Organic debris, moisture-related growth, rust sources, spills, and pool-area contaminants can all affect travertine differently.
