Why Jacksonville Pressure Washing Matters

In Northeast Florida, surfaces can look worn quickly when heat and humidity combine with rain cycles, mildew, algae, and pollen. Regular pressure washing helps keep exterior areas cleaner and better maintained before buildup becomes severe.

  • Driveways: Collect dirt, algae, tire marks, and storm runoff.
  • Patios and walkways: Can get slippery and dark from shade and moisture.
  • Pool decks: Stay exposed to splash-out, chemicals, and heavy use.
  • House exteriors: Pick up mildew, pollen, and everyday grime.
  • Routine cleaning: Helps maintain curb appeal and a better-kept look.

How often should exterior surfaces be pressure washed in Florida?

Most homes benefit from exterior cleaning every 6 to 12 months. Timing depends on shade, moisture, tree coverage, pollen, and how quickly mildew or algae returns.
