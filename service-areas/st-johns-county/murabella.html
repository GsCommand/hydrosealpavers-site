Restoring Murabella’s Warm-Toned Pavers

Our Professional 6 Step Process

Our six-step process is tailored to Murabella’s older warm-toned pavers and transformation-focused restoration goals.

  1. Inspection: review traffic patterns, drainage, and existing paver condition
  2. Clean: use controlled surface cleaning and targeted treatment where needed
  3. Re-sand: install and compact kiln-dried joint sand for proper lockup
  4. Dry & verify: confirm moisture levels and weather window before sealing
  5. Seal: apply even coats with clean transitions and consistent finish level
  6. Final walkthrough: provide cure-time guidance and maintenance recommendations
