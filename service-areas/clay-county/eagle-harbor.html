How do I know if my Eagle Harbor pavers need cleaning, sanding, or full resealing?

If the pavers look faded, the joints seem weak, dark buildup is collecting, or water no longer beads evenly across the surface, it may be time for service. Some Eagle Harbor hardscapes mainly need cleaning and fresh sand, while others need full resealing to restore a cleaner finish and stronger protection. We inspect the condition first so the recommendation matches the actual surface.

How often should pavers be resealed in Eagle Harbor?

Most Eagle Harbor paver surfaces benefit from resealing every 2 to 3 years. Timing depends on exposure, traffic, pool use, and how quickly the finish wears down in visible outdoor areas. Higher-use and more exposed surfaces may need service sooner.
