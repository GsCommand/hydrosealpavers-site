Paver Protection for Pool Decks and Outdoor Areas in Eagle Harbor

Large entertainment areas and pool surrounds require consistent prep and controlled application. Our six-step process is designed to keep transitions clean and finish levels even across connected outdoor spaces, including proper joint service and sand selection (see sand options).

  1. Inspection: site-specific traffic, drainage, and exposure review
  2. Clean: targeted chemistry and controlled surface cleaning
  3. Re-sand: install and compact kiln-dried joint sand
  4. Dry & verify: moisture checks and weather-window confirmation
  5. Seal: even application with controlled finish level
  6. Final walkthrough: cure timeline, care notes, and reseal guidance
