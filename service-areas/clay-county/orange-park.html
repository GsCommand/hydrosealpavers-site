How do I know if my Orange Park pavers need cleaning, sanding, or full resealing?

If your pavers in Orange Park look dull, have loose joint sand, show dark staining, or seem to absorb water quickly, it may be time for restoration work. Most Orange Park paver surfaces do best on a 2 to 3 year reseal cycle. Timing depends on tree cover, moisture, traffic, and how quickly the existing finish breaks down. Older driveways and shaded patios may need attention sooner than more protected areas.
