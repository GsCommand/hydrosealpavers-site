What are the signs that my Queens Harbour pavers need to be resealed?

Signs include faded color, loose or missing joint sand, dark buildup, white haze, slippery areas, and water soaking into the pavers instead of beading up. In Queens Harbour, moisture and coastal exposure can cause pavers to wear faster over time.

How often should pavers be resealed in Queens Harbour?

Most Queens Harbour homes benefit from resealing every 2 to 3 years. Areas with more moisture, stronger sun, or heavier outdoor use may need service sooner to maintain protection and appearance.
