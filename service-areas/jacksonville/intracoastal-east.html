We restore the surface and lock in protection before those areas start looking permanently stained or patchy.

  • Helps limit mildew buildup on shaded walkways and around screened pool cages

Moisture and Outdoor-Living Wear in Intracoastal East

Pavers in Intracoastal East usually stay damp longer than inland neighborhoods, so wear shows up differently and often faster around shaded, low-drainage, and poolside areas.

  • Mildew buildup near fences, preserve lines, and screened enclosures
  • Washed-out joint sand near drains, pool decks, and low spots
  • Darker patches that never seem to fully dry between rains
  • Slippery surfaces around pool areas and long-shade sections
  • Uneven color where older sealer has worn off in patches
